--- layout: default title: Home --- Welcome! This site is intended as a repository of high-quality collegiate quizbowl packets for team practice and individual study. To get started, check out some of the sample packets below!

Novice

Novice tournaments are designed for players new to collegiate quizbowl, or even quizbowl entirely.
  • 2017 ACF Fall
  • 2019 ACF Fall
  • 2012 Early Autumn Collegiate Novice

Medium

Medium-difficulty tournaments are designed to be played by competitors of all skill levels.
  • 2019 Early Fall Tournament
  • 2017 Early Fall Tournament
  • 2014 Minnesota Undergraduate Tournament

Regional

Regional-difficulty tournaments serve as qualifiers for national tournaments. Players looking to step up from novice and medium-difficulty tournaments will find these tournaments helpful.
  • 2020 ACF Regionals
  • 2018 ACF Regionals
  • 2017 ACF Regionals

Nationals and Open

These tournaments are the most difficult on the collegiate calendar, and are suitable for teams and players preparing for nationals.
  • 2019 Fall Open
  • 2019 ACF Nationals
  • 2018 ACF Nationals
